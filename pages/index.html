Summit Hotel -Naran-

Enjoy a peaceful escape at Summit Hotel, located just a 2-minute walk from Naran Bazaar. Our upscale hotel offers beautifully furnished 5-star rooms with modern amenities and stunning mountain views.

Relax in a serene setting away from the noise, while staying close to local shops, food stalls, and souvenir spots. Whether you're here to unwind or explore, Summit Hotel offers the perfect balance of comfort and convenience.

Rooms

Experience comfort and serenity in our premium rooms Our spacious, beautifully furnished rooms offer a homely atmosphere with all the modern comforts you need. Enjoy 24/7 electricity powered by a backup generator and hot running water around the clock. Each room is thoroughly sanitized and kept spotless for your peace of mind. Many rooms feature private balconies—perfect for relaxing with family while soaking in breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. With modern, clean bathrooms, secure locks for privacy, and unmatched space compared to our competitors, your stay will be both comfortable and memorable

Restaurant

Our in-house restaurant welcomes all guests to enjoy freshly prepared meals, crafted with care and held to the highest standards of cleanliness and nourishment. Floor-to-ceiling glass windows offer breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valley, creating a truly serene dining experience. With dedicated staff available at all times, you can expect attentive, seamless service—ensuring your needs are met promptly and allowing you to focus entirely on the meal and the moment.
