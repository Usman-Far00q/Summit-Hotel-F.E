Description

This well-appointed room features one double bed and one single bed, with ample space to comfortably add two floor mattresses. One mattress is complimentary; a second can be provided upon request at an additional charge. The room includes a private attached bathroom, accessible only to the guests of the room, and is equipped with round-the-clock hot water and complimentary amenities such as shampoo and dental kits. Uninterrupted electricity is ensured, supported by a backup generator for added reliability.
